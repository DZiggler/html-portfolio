John Gotti

John Gotti, boss of the Gambino crime family, became known as "The Teflon Don" because charges against him repeatedly failed to stick. His flamboyance and public image made him one of the most famous mobsters in history.

Crimes He Was Convicted Of

  • Murder
  • Racketeering
  • Loan sharking
  • Illegal gambling
  • Conspiracy

Impact on Organized Crime

  1. Increased FBI pressure on the Mafia due to his media exposure.
  2. Oversaw one of the wealthiest and most powerful crime families in the U.S.
  3. Brought unwanted public attention to organized crime.

Other Mobsters

  • Lucky Luciano
  • Vincent Gigante
  • Frank Costello
  • Carlo Gambino
