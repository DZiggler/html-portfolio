Carlo Gambino

Carlo Gambino was the long-time head of the Gambino crime family, known for being quiet, strategic, and low-profile. Under his leadership, the family became the most powerful Mafia family in America.

Crimes He Was Convicted Of

  • Illegal gambling
  • Tax violations
  • (Most crimes suspected but never proven in court)

Impact on Organized Crime

  1. Turned the Gambino family into the dominant force in the Mafia.
  2. Promoted subtle, business-focused leadership rather than violence.
  3. Helped form long-lasting alliances across crime families.
