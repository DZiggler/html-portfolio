Lucky Luciano

Lucky Luciano, born Salvatore Lucania, was an Italian-American mobster and crime boss. He is considered the father of modern organized crime in the United States. Luciano played a major role in establishing the Mafia's nationwide structure and operations. He was a key figure in transforming organized crime into a highly organized, business-like enterprise.

Crimes He Was Convicted Of

  • Prostitution
  • Racketeering
  • Murder conspiracy
  • Illegal gambling operations

Impact on Organized Crime

  1. Created the Commission to govern Mafia families and reduce internal conflicts.
  2. Structured organized crime as a hierarchical and business-oriented system.
  3. Expanded international influence through alliances with crime syndicates overseas.

Other Mobsters

  • Vincent Gigante
  • Frank Costello
  • John Gotti
  • Carlo Gambino
