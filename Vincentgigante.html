Vincent Gigante

Vincent "The Chin" Gigante was the boss of the Genovese crime family and one of the most powerful Mafia leaders in American history. Known for faking mental illness—wandering Greenwich Village in a bathrobe— he avoided prosecution for decades.

Crimes He Was Convicted Of

  • Racketeering
  • Extortion
  • Conspiracy to commit murder
  • Obstruction of justice

Impact on Organized Crime

  1. Ran the Genovese family with strict secrecy and discipline.
  2. Used psychological deception to evade law enforcement.
  3. Maintained the family's power through controlled infiltration of unions.
