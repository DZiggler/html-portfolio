Frank Costello

Frank Costello, known as "The Prime Minister of the Underworld," was a powerhouse within the American Mafia and a major figure in national politics. He rose to leadership of the Luciano (later Genovese) crime family.

Crimes He Was Convicted Of

  • Tax evasion
  • Illegal gambling
  • Racketeering (suspected but not convicted)

Impact on Organized Crime

  1. Influenced American politics through bribery and corruption.
  2. Encouraged the Mafia to be low-profile and business-oriented.
  3. Built extensive gambling operations across the U.S.

Other Mobsters

  • Lucky Luciano
  • Vincent Gigante
  • John Gotti
  • Carlo Gambino
